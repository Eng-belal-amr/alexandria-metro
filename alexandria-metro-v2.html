WHY THE METRO

A City Transformed

The metro replaces the aging Abu Qir suburban railway with a high-capacity electric rapid transit system.

⚡
Fully Electric
Zero direct emissions. Clean energy powering 189 railcars across 21.7 km.
⏱
2.5 Min Headway
Trains every 2.5 minutes at peak, down from 10-minute intervals on the old railway.
🚄
Half Travel Time
End-to-end in 25 minutes instead of 50. Reliable, on-time, every time.
🏗
Elevated Design
14 of 20 stations elevated — removing dangerous crossings, freeing street space.
🇪🇬
Made in Egypt
All 21 trains built by NERIC in East Port Said — building Egypt's rail industry.
🌍
International Backing
Backed by EIB, EBRD, AFD, AIIB — European engineering standards throughout.
